## 💡 Overview

In the rapidly evolving digital ecosystem, access to accurate and timely data from diverse platforms is paramount for competitive advantage. Our Bot API service is designed to address this need by offering tailored data acquisition solutions that span e-commerce, social media, video content, and food delivery platforms. We accurately capture, organize, and output data within specified scopes, enabling highly efficient market research, customer development, competitor analysis, and content strategy optimization.

## 🌐 Supported Platforms and Key Features

### 1. Shopee (E-commerce Data) 🛍️
- Store Details: Comprehensive metrics for individual Shopee stores  
- Product Details: Specifications, pricing, and historical performance  
- Category & Keyword Data: Aggregated data for product categories and search keywords  
- Price & Sales Performance: Track pricing strategies, sales volumes, and revenue trends  
- Review & Rating Data: Collect customer feedback for sentiment analysis  
- New Arrivals & Changes: Monitor new listings and modifications  

### 2. TikTok (Social Media & Short-Form Video Data) 🎶
- User Profiles: Public profile info, followers, and engagement metrics  
- Video Details: Metadata, views, likes, comments, shares, trending status  
- Hashtag & Trend Analysis: Popular hashtags, trending topics, associated content  
- Comment & Engagement: Analyze user comments and interactions  
- Ad Campaign Insights: Public ad creatives and performance  

### 3. Facebook (Social Media & Community Data) 📘
- Page & Group Insights: Public page/group activity, engagement metrics  
- Post Performance: Metrics for individual posts  
- User Demographics: Aggregated, anonymized public data  
- Ad Library Data: Competitor analysis using Facebook Ad Library  
- Event Data: Public events and engagement  

### 4. YouTube (Video Content & Creator Data) ▶️
- Channel Analytics: Public channel statistics, subscriber growth  
- Video Performance: Views, likes, comments, engagement rates  
- Keyword & Topic Trends: Popular search terms, video topics  
- Comment Sentiment Analysis: Audience sentiment on videos  
- Competitor Channel Monitoring: Track competitor strategies  

### 5. KeeTa (Food Delivery Data) 🍜
- Restaurant Details: Location, hours, ratings  
- Menu Data: Items, prices, ingredients, specials  
- Pricing & Promotions: Discounts, offers, real-time pricing  
- Delivery Logistics: Times, fees, service areas  
- Customer Reviews: User ratings for dishes  
- Order Patterns: Trends in popular dishes and order frequency
